Game Objective

When all cities are occupied, the player who occupied the most cities wins.

Game Flow

1. The four golden areas represent the four kingdoms, each containing the resources of the kings.

2. Each player takes turns rolling a die and moves according to the result.

3. Each new round of conquest for a king begins from the city they last captured.

4. If a player lands on an unoccupied city, they can choose to spend a certain amount of resources to claim it.

5. If a player lands on a city occupied by another player, they must offer a higher resource to take over the city or choose to engage in special events.

6. Number 1 is the lowest value, and queen is the highest.

7. When the queen is guarding a city, other kings cannot activate any of their special abilities.

Special Abilities

King of Clubs

Skill - Conquest Expansion: When passing through a city occupied by another king, they can initiate a duel (Roll the die; choose to bet on high or low. If successful, they gain ownership of the city by paying any one of the resources. )

King of Hearts

Skill - Alliance Power: Upon occupying 3 neighboring cities or above, they will permanently own these cities.

King of Spades

Skill - Oracle Insight: Gains two rolls during their turn, with movement determined by the second roll.

King of Diamonds

Skill - Wealth Accumulation: Can seize any one resource from the opponent, but cannot get the ownership of the city where the two sides meet, nor from the resources of the captured cities, these captured cards are only owned and cannot be used. When Jake or Queen occupy a city, this ability is nullified, and also cannot seize the opponent's Jake and Queen.
